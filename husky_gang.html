About Us

The Husky Gang is a group of students dedicated to making classrooms a better place. We work to minimize disruptions, ensure smooth learning experiences, and support our teachers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to prevent unnecessary disruptions in the classroom and help teachers focus on what they do best: teaching. We believe in creating an environment where everyone can learn and grow.
